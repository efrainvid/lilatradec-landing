About Us

At Lilatradec, we are dedicated to the production and export of the highest quality roses. Our passion for perfection is reflected in every flower, guaranteeing freshness, vibrant colors, and a long vase life. "Open your wings to the world" is not just our motto, it's our promise to bring the beauty of our flowers to every corner of the planet.
